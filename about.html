About

ILXS is formed with a vision to provide solutions for digital documentation for the defence sector. We focus on continuous development for products which will be compatible with atleast a decade old hardware systems. We deliver all the levels of Interactive Electronic Technical Manual (IETM) and S1000D documentation as per customer requirement.



We at ILXS offer solutions like IETM, Computer Based Training (CBT), Exploded views, etc. Our team comprises of experienced engineers with experience in fields of Mechanical, Electronics, Pneumatics and Hydraulics. We create quality output with project understanding leading to user friendly solution development. Our team also consist of experienced professionals from the field of technical documentation.



All our products comply to documentation standards JSS, JSG, MIL and S1000D. Pledging with Make in India initiative by GoI, we are striving to be the leaders in providing documentation solution which will help to significantly reduce maintainence time for complex defence systems.
